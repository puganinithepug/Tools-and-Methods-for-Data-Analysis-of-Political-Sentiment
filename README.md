# Data Analsis of Political Sentiment and Topic Distribution
**Political Climate Analysis of Zohran Mamdani's 2025 New York Elections**

This project is a team-based analysis on the Political climate of Zohran Mamdani's 2025 New York Elections.
For raw data, the analysis draws on news articles from a news provider (www.thenewsapi.com).

To inform the analysis, 500 articles were collected on the topic of Zohran Mamdani's 2025 New York Elections from North American news outlets.

Topics for study were developed through open coding sessions on 200 articles, classifying each article to belong to exactly one topic.  
Once the topics were designed, team-members annotated the total dataset, no double annoation or ambigous categories were permitted.
Similarly, every article was assigned positive, negative or neutral sentiment, following an established rarionale developed by the team.

Conclusively, topics were charcaterized by (1) computing the 10 words in each category with the highest tf-idf scores and (2) leveraging an LLM to produce a representative summary of each category.
