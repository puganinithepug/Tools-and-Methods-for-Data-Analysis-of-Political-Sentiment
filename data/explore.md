How big is the dataset?
4.7MB is the file size, the length of the file is 36860 lines.
What’s the structure of the data? (i.e., what are the field and what are values in them)
The title of the episode, the writer of the episode and what character is speaking (narrator, one of the ponies, several ponies), and finally the dialog, what the speaker is saying.
How many episodes does it cover?
There are 196 unique titles in the data set for different episodes. Therefore, the dataset covers 196 episodes.

The fact that sometimes there are several speakers for a dialog at once.
